I helped someone make a poster today about a book called “Outliers” by Malcolm Gladwell. They were making it about the theme of dedication and I started wondering what I am dedicated to. I thought about it and decided it was pretty clear my dedicated task was to help people write! It is my only real work. If I didn’t dedicate my time to it what else would I be doing??? I think its important that everyone is dedicated to at least something. Without dedication to a craft how will you ever be successful at it? I know if I wasn’t writing all the time I wouldn’t be so good at it. <\/p>\n\n\n\n
